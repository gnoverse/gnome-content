Getting Started with Gno Studio Connect
Gno Studio Connect is your gateway to managing and deploying realms on the Gno.land network. Here’s a quick guide to help you get acquainted with its features.

1. Sign Up and Log In
Visit: Go to Gno Studio Connect.
Sign Up: If you’re new, click on “Sign Up” and follow the registration steps.
Log In: Enter your credentials to access the dashboard.
2. Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in, you’ll land on the dashboard. Here’s what you’ll find:

Realms Management: View, deploy, and manage all your realms.
Connections: Manage connections to different Gno chains (local, testnet, mainnet).
Activity Logs: Monitor recent activities, deployments, and executions.
3. Connecting to a Gno Chain
To interact with a Gno chain:

Click on “Add Connection”.

Select Chain Type: Choose between local, testnet, or mainnet.

Enter Details:

Chain ID
RPC URL
Account Information
Save and Test: Ensure the connection is active by testing it.

4. Deploying a Realm
Deploying a realm is straightforward:

Navigate to “Realms”.
Click “Deploy New Realm”.
Upload or Paste Your Code.
Configure Settings: Name your realm, add descriptions, and select the target chain.
Deploy: Click the deploy button and monitor the process via activity logs.
5. Executing a Realm
To run your deployed realm:

Select the Realm from the “Realms” list.
Go to the “Execute” Tab.
Run the Realm: Click “Run” and view the output in real-time.
6. Monitoring and Logs
Stay on top of your deployments and executions:

Activity Logs: Check all recent actions, including deployments and executions.
Error Logs: Troubleshoot any issues by reviewing error messages and logs.
7. Additional Features
Version Control: Manage different versions of your realms.
Collaboration Tools: Share access with team members for collaborative development.
Analytics: Gain insights into realm performance and usage.
